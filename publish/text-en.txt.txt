The Tomato technique is a time management technology that can be used for any tasks. The Tomato technique provides a simple tool for improving productivity:
* Facilitates getting started
* Improves concentration, getting rid of distractions
* Improves understanding of your current goal
* Allows you to break the overall task into smaller steps
* Improves the task assessment process
* Allows you to keep motivation
* Allows you to distribute time for work and rest
